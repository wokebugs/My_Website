Introduction

Hello! My name is Dannica but I go by Dani or Dan. I am a 20 year old Filipino-American that grew up in a military lifestyle. Until 2019, I have been moving around the majority of my life. I was born in Yokosuka, Japan where I first spent the first 2-3 years of my life. I then moved to San Diego, Califirnia and lived there for 2 years. Afterwards, my family and I moved back to Japan, this time in Misawa and stayed there for 4 years. After spending a small amount of my elementary there, we moved to Mississippi where I spent the majority of elementary schooling, and lived there for about 3 years. Afterwards, we moved to Napoli, Italy and spent my middleschool and first 1 and a half years of high-school at and I lived there for 4 and a half years. After living overseas for a majority of my life, we decided to settle in Washington!


After spending many years in many different environments, I picked up a few interests and hobbies!
