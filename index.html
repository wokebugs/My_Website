Dannica's Stickers!

I have been interested in collecting stickers ever since I was a kid and got inspired by my sister. I currently own hundreds of sticker sheets and many singular stickers as well!

I started collecting stickers as a hobby last year. I try to attend as many events as possible that would be selling these goods. I recently bought a bunch of stickers from Sakura-Con 2024! I spend about $80 on stickers, using them to give out as gifts, decorate my personal items such as my phone, laptop, and notebooks, as well as decorating personalized letters.

What I use stickers for!

  • I like to decorate things such as my phone, notebooks, and my laptop
  • While journaling, I like to decorate the pages so they don't look too bland
  • I think stickers give items more personality and it's fun to customize personal things with them

Where I get my stickers!

  • Daiso
  • Kinokuniya
  • Small businesses from events such as Sakura-Con, PAX, and Maker's Fair as well as Etsy
