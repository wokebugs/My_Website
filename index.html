I have been interested in collecting stickers ever since I was a kid and got inspired by my sister. I currently own hundreds of sticker sheets and many many singualar stickers as well!

What I use stickers for!

  • I like to decorate things such as my phone, notebooks, and my laptop
  • While journaling, I like to decorate the pages so they don't look too bland
  • I think stickers give items more personality and it's fun to customize personal things with them

Where I get my stickers!

  • Daiso
  • Kinokuniya
  • Small buisinesses from events such as Sakura-Con, PAX, and Maker's Fair as well as Etsy

you can also make some yourself by buying sticker sheets and printing designs out at home!
