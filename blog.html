Sakura-Con 2024 ღゝ◡╹)ノ♡

I recently went to Sakura-con! It took place at the Seattle Convention Center . There were SOOO many vendors! The con was held in two different buildings: Summit and Arch at 705 Pike.

There were vendors selling anime merch and goodies, props, clothes, but most importantly, stickers.

My favorite stickers that I ended up buying were three car peekers from Ahhgela. They were pretty reasonably priced and I believe there was a buy 3 for $15. Considering the price and quality, it was a REALLY good deal. I ended up buying Miffy, Tomagotchi Mametchi, and NewJeans decals for my car. I haven't put them on my car yet though but plan to soon!
